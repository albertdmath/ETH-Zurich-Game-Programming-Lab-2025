Fatal Ovation is a 2-4 player battle royale party game. You're jesters and have just finished your performance, but the audience does not seem impressed. The angry mob is slowly closing in and they are throwing a variety of things at you. Some projectiles are harmful while others might provide useful power-ups and protection. Dodge, catch, and throw the incoming projectiles of various kinds onto your fellow jesters and be the last man standing. Even if you fall, you can still become part of the mob and take revenge upon your former comrades. Make sure this is not your last performance!
